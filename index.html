This is a html5 template created by Chris Shilts, called Style Blue. It features a responsive, mobile-first design. Go ahead and resize the window.

Notice how the image resizes automatically, to fit the screen. This is achieved by giving images a max-width of 100%.

Of course, we also need to take into consideration other content, such as videos. Take the following Youtube video for example. You might think it would be as simple as applying a width of 100% to iframe. But in actuality it's abit more complicated (see source code).
